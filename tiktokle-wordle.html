About TikTokle Wordle

TikTokle Wordle.

In ten attempts, guess the tiktoker. The color of the tiles will change after each guess to illustrate how close your guess was to the tiktoker name. For each guess, you may enter any sequence of characters.

How do you use tiktokle wordle?
1 The goal of the game is to figure out where the hidden tiktoker is. To begin, type a word with the specified number of letters in the first line.
2 After you've entered the word, press the Enter key on the virtual keyboard. Take note of the color used to highlight the letters now.
3 If the number is not colored and remains gray, there is no such number in the secret phone nuble. If the number is highlighted in yellow, it is in the concealed word but is in a different cell. If a number is highlighted in green, it is in a hidden word in this cell.
4
Please keep in mind that letters in a word might be repeated; for example, if a number is highlighted in yellow or green, it can appear in the word once or several times.
5 Now, on the second line, type the following word and hit Enter. Take into account the information about the letters and their placement gathered in the first round when doing so.
6
Continue typing phrases in successive lines until you figure out where the hidden tiktoker is. After finishing the game, share the outcome with your friends. Most importantly, you can only play once per day. Everyone has the same phone number.
